Last updated: November 24, 2025

Arivya (“we”, “us”, or “our”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the Arivya mobile application (“App”).

1. Information We Collect

Arivya is designed with privacy in mind. We only collect the minimum information necessary to provide core functionality such as account creation, syncing content, and keeping your experience secure.

We may collect:

  • Basic account information (e.g., email address if you choose to create an account)

We do not collect your personal notes, thoughts, or any content you enter in the app for advertising or marketing purposes.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect or store:

  • Contacts
  • Photos or media from your device
  • Precise location data
  • Health data
  • Payment card information
  • Advertising identifiers for targeted ads

3. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Provide and personalize your experience in the app
  • Enable optional account-based features
  • Maintain security and prevent misuse
  • Ensure app stability, performance, and reliability

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes.

4. Data Storage and Security

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and physical measures designed to protect your data. Your account information (if you choose to create one) is securely stored and used only for providing the app’s features.

5. Children’s Privacy

Arivya is not directed to children under the age required to provide consent in their region. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has created an account, please contact us so we can assist with removal.

6. Your Choices

You may choose to:

  • Use the app without creating an account (where available)
  • Delete your account and associated data by contacting us
  • Disable notifications from your device settings
